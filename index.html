Sobre mim

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by people who can turn ideas into impact. When I was ten, watching The Social Network with my father, I realized something that shaped my path: the power of knowledge and technology to change the world. That moment sparked a desire to master logic, understand systems, and build something meaningful, not through chaos, but through creation.

Today, that ambition has become my purpose. I'm a Full-Stack Developer since 2023, programming in Java (Spring Boot) and TypeScript using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js). In recent years, I've built systems that connect technology and strategy, including Isteel, a modular platform integrating clients, metalworkers, and drivers, and ForBra, a geolocation-based app with JWT authentication designed to connect Brazilians abroad. I'm driven by curiosity, purpose, and innovation, because every line of code has the potential to create lasting impact.
